Negative Effects of Fossil Fuels

Fossil fuels, such as coal, oil, and natural gas, are major contributors to air and water pollution, greenhouse gas emissions, and climate change. Burning fossil fuels releases harmful pollutants into the atmosphere, leading to respiratory problems, acid rain, and smog. Additionally, the extraction and transportation of fossil fuels can result in habitat destruction, water contamination, and wildlife endangerment.
